Basic Steps

1

Read Application Instructions

Ensure you read the details and instructions for the specific recruitment application which you want to apply

2

Register Your Portal Account

Register from the job page to create an account for access to proceed with your recruitment application steps

3

Validate registered Account

When you register, you may be required to validate your account by clicking on the link that will be sent to your provided email address

4

Login Your Account

After validation, you will be required to login your newly created account to continue with the application process

5

Fill The Form

Fill the form, supply all required fields, provide all relevant information and also upload all relevant documents.

6

Submit Your Application

After filling the form, verify all your details carefully from the summary page, submit your application and print slip
